Volunteer

We firmly believe that service to humanity is the best work of life. Our volunteers are people who are committed to make a difference for others. We are fun mix of those who have grown up from homes and institutions themselves, college students, industry professionals and many other interesting people. Volunteers have had a lot of fun organizing events, interacting with the children and most importantly witnessing them discover their potential.

By volunteering with us:

  • You will be sharing your learnings and life lessons with those who are lesser privileged than you.


  • It will help you ensure that visible change is being made.


  • You will be gaining leadership, humanitarian and social skills.

Volunteering opportunities with Our Children include:

  • Participating in and organizing events for children like storytelling, outings, excursions

  • Coaching children in studies at institutions

  • Organize/teach/assist in art and craft activities

  • Mentor, provide career guidance, and teach life skills

  • Be a part of our incredibly enriching programs like Learning Beyond
