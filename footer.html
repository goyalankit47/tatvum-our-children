Founded in 1970, Our Children partners with over 35 Child Welfare Institutions in Mumbai to run development programs and activities. Our Children (OC) acts as a bridge between a child in an institution and the outside world, opening up opportunities which enable children to grow, learn and succeed in facing the challenging world ahead. OC’s mantra has been to provide every Child with a chance to be a Child and we follow a very simple philosophy - Strive to provide as many opportunities as possible to as many children as possible.
