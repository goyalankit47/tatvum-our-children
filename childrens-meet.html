Over 2000 children from institutions all over Mumbai take part in a cultural program giving them an opportunity to display their creativity and talent using music, dance and art. We organize this program to provide a platform to children to exhibit their hidden talents and engage in creative activities. The platform also gives them exposure to be with other children and know they are not alone. Also on display are artworks from drawing contests held in the month before the event. A unique feature of this program is that the event is organised by the children for the children and even the Chief Guests are invited from amongst the children.
