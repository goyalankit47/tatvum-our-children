Donate

Even a small contribution from you can make a huge difference in someone’s life.
While creating an evident impact on the lives of 4000+ children, we have earned the reputation of a trusted and credible organization that is transparent in its workings.

By donating to Our Children:
You will be an active participant in affecting change in the lives of lesser privileged children.
It will help you channel your good intentions into actually making a difference.
You will be able to witness the changes that your donations are making.

We utilize our funds to pay for children’s education, health, scholarships, running group homes and organizing camps and activities. With most of Our Children being volunteer driven, our administrative costs are razor thin and the money you give goes to the cause.
You can donate by filling out the given form: Donations to Our Children are exempt under section 80 (G) of Income Tax Act. We are also registered under Foreign Contribution (Regulation) Act 2010, eligible to receive Foreign Donations

All online donations are secured and handled by our PCI DSS compliant partner Instamojo

Volunteer

We firmly believe that service to humanity is the best work of life. Our volunteers are people who are committed to make a difference for others. We are fun mix of those who have grown up from homes and institutions themselves, college students, industry professionals and many other interesting people. Volunteers have had a lot of fun organizing events, interacting with the children and most importantly witnessing them discover their potential.

By volunteering with us:

  • You will be sharing your learnings and life lessons with those who are lesser privileged than you.


  • It will help you ensure that visible change is being made.


  • You will be gaining leadership, humanitarian and social skills.

Volunteering opportunities with Our Children include:

  • Participating in and organizing events for children like storytelling, outings, excursions

  • Coaching children in studies at institutions

  • Organize/teach/assist in art and craft activities

  • Mentor, provide career guidance, and teach life skills

  • Be a part of our incredibly enriching programs like Learning Beyond

Other NGOs:.
Partnering with other organizations to create change has been a preferred mode of operation for us. Our trusted relationships with child welfare organizations built over the course of 47 years makes us a natural partner to channel your good intentions and expertise and jointly bring a change into the lives of others. Some of of our successful partnerships include Dental Camps for Children (with Angel Dentist Network), Outings For Children (with Powai Ladies Association), Birthday Celebrations (With Rotary Club) and many more..

Artists/ Musicians:
We have partnered with organizations like “Magicians Without Borders” or “Mime A Lot” who have taken incredible effort in interacting with the students and teaching them skills. Additionally, we have had painters and choreographers conduct classes for the children. This also inspires children to take it up as a profession.
